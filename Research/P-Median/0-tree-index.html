Trees used as Examples for P-Median Algorithms

  • ce-3_med_convex.graphml - counterexample to conjecture that the 2-medians are within the subtree defined by the 3-medians;
    another way to put it: each two median is on a path between two 3-medians (including endpoints)

  • ce-subtree.graphml - counterexample to conjecture that best cut edge is in one of the two heaviest subtrees

  • ce-binary.graphml - binary tree counterexample to the same conjecture
  • ce-my-2-med.graphml - counterexample to my-2-median.alg, which relies on the conjecture
